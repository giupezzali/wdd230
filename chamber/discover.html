Central Fair

Next to Central Park is the Central Fair, where local merchants gather to offer their products. The fair gathers more than 3,000 people every Saturday, making this fair one of the most attractive for merchants.

The perfect place for farmers!

Among the products offered at the central fair are fruits and vegetables from the area, processed foods, and handicrafts. It is the preferred place for farmers and those who sell food due to the large concentration of people.
